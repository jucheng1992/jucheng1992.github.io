About me

I am a fourth year Ph.D. candidate in Interdepartmental Group in Biostatistics at UC Berkeley,
with emphasis in Computational Science and Engineer. My advisor is Prof. Mark van der Laan.
My research interests mainly focus on machine learning and causal inference.

Before coming to Berkeley in Aug 2014, I received my B.S. degree of Statistics in Southeast
University, China, where I worked as a research assistant in Research Center for Complex Systems
and Network Sciences, Southeast University under the supervision of Prof. Jinde Cao.
