About me

I am a second year Ph.D. student in Interdepartmental Group in Biostatistics. My advisor is Mark van der Laan. My research interests mainly focus on machine learning and causal inference.

Before coming to Berkeley in Aug 2014, I received my B.S. degree of Statistics in Southeast University, China, where I worked as a research assistant in Research Center for Complex Systems and Network Sciences, Southeast University under the supervision of Prof. Jinde Cao.
